Frequently Asked Questions

Dues for the year are $150.00. Dues for one semester are $75.00. If you are accepted in the Spring Semester, you are only expected to pay dues for one semester!

With your dues, you get:

  • Free Boarderline Stickers and Bandanas
  • Discounts on Ikon and Massanutten Passes
  • Housing on Snowshoe Trips
  • Access to all of our social events
  • Invites to our big trips!

For the 2023-2024 school year, Boarderline is approximately 350 members.

Yes. We have a point system for the club where members have to participate in certain involvements to get points. These involvements include cleaning after social events, going to meeting, participating in our community service, and more! This system ensures our available spots are given to our most dedicated members. Each year level as a minimum point requirement each semester. If you do not reach your point requirement, you will be removed from the club. But don't fret! Its super easy and fun to get your points!

Unfortunatly, we cannot let every member on our Snowshoe trips. Acceptance on the trips is based on our point system. We accept members who have the most points on the trips first. The more you do for the club, the more points you get, and the more likely you will be accepted to go on the Snowshoe trips!
