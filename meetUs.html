Meet Our Executive Team


Riley Wise

President

Home Mountain: Snowshoe, West Virginia

"I am from Stafford Va, and I am a senior here at JMU. I have only been snowboarding for 3 seasons now, because I did not learn how until freshman year when i taught myself. No one in my family skis or boards, so I was never exposed to it as a kid. I came to JMU hoping to try new things, and that is exactly what I did. Around this same time, I joined Boarderline with a few friends just because it looked cool, and was hoping to make more friends along the way. It quickly became much more than that. Boarderline has since become not only my JMU family, but also where I spend most of my time. Being able to make new friends and spend time doing something new that I loved, has been such a great experience. I have made tons of memories through Boarderline so far and I am super excited to be the president during my 4th year in the club!!!"

"A fun fact about me is 3 out of 5 of my concussions are already from snowboarding"
